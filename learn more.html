Learn More About EverGreen Empire

1. Our Mission

At EverGreen Empire, we believe that creators should not just be able to express their creativity but also earn from it. We provide a platform for creators to license premium music tracks, while receiving royalties for the content they produce using our music.

2. What We Offer

EverGreen Empire offers the following:

  • Premium Music: Access a wide variety of music tracks suitable for video creators, gamers, influencers, and content producers.
  • Creator Program: Join our program and start earning royalties by using our music in your content. We offer 30% royalties for every track used.
  • Easy Dashboard: Track your earnings, performance, and statistics through our intuitive and transparent dashboard.

3. How It Works

Joining our platform is simple:

  • Sign Up: Create an account and get verified by our team.
  • Choose Tracks: Browse our extensive catalog of music and select tracks that fit your content.
  • License and Use: Use the music in your videos or streams. We handle the royalty collection and payout.
  • Earn Royalties: Receive monthly payments based on the usage of the tracks in your content.

4. Benefits for Creators

As a creator, you get:

  • Ownership: Retain 30% of the revenue generated from your videos using our music.
  • Transparency: Full visibility on your earnings, royalties, and track usage.
  • Long-Term Collaboration: Work with EverGreen Empire for long-term opportunities and a sustainable revenue stream.

5. Why Choose EverGreen Empire?

We’re different because we care about creators and their needs. Our platform was designed with the creator in mind, providing:

  • Fair Revenue Sharing: We believe in a fair 30% royalty split.
  • Easy Integration: Quickly integrate our music into your content with minimal hassle.
  • Community Support: Our team is dedicated to helping creators succeed and grow.

6. Get Started

Ready to turn your views into royalties? Join the EverGreen Empire Creator Program today and start earning!
